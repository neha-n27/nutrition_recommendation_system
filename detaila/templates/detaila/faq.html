What are the sources of Vitamin A?

Sources: Sweet potatoes, carrots, dark leafy greens.

What are the symptoms of Vitamin C deficiency?

Deficiencies may lead to scurvy, fatigue, weakness, bleeding gums, and impaired wound healing.

What are the best sources of Calcium?

Sources: Dairy products, leafy greens, nuts, seeds, and fortified plant-based milk.

What is the recommended daily intake of calcium?

The recommended daily intake of calcium varies by age, but most adults need around 1,000 mg per day.

What is the function of potassium in the body?

Potassium helps regulate fluid balance, blood pressure, and supports healthy heart function.

How much sodium should I consume daily?

The American Heart Association recommends consuming no more than 2,300 mg of sodium per day.

How can I increase my intake of omega-3 fatty acids?

Eat fatty fish like salmon, sardines, and mackerel, or take a fish oil supplement.

What are healthy fats?

Healthy fats include unsaturated fats found in nuts, seeds, avocados, and olive oil, which support heart health and provide energy.

How can I reduce my sugar intake?

Limit sugary drinks, read food labels, choose unsweetened products, and opt for natural sources like fruits and vegetables.

How much water should I drink daily?

Aim for at least 8 cups (64 ounces) of water per day, but individual needs may vary depending on age, sex, weight, and activity level.

What is a balanced diet?

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods from all food groups in appropriate portions, providing essential nutrients for optimal health.

What is the difference between saturated and unsaturated fats?

Saturated fats (found in animal products and processed meats) can increase cholesterol levels, while unsaturated fats (found in nuts, seeds, and avocados) can lower them.

What is the recommended daily intake of fiber?

The daily recommended intake of fiber is 25-30 grams for adults, but most people consume only about 15 grams per day.

What are the benefits of a high-fiber diet?

A high-fiber diet can help with weight management, lower cholesterol levels, regulate blood sugar, and promote regular bowel movements.
